Raffi — Support

A gentle nudge to sip water, with a giraffe who remembers for you.

How Raffi works

Set your active window and how often you'd like a nudge, and Raffi waves now and then during your day. When you drink, tap "I had a sip." That's the whole app — no goals, no charts.

Reminders aren't showing up

Raffi needs notification permission to nudge you. If you skipped it at first launch, open the iOS Settings app, find Raffi, tap Notifications, and turn Allow Notifications on.

Changing the timing

Open the Reminders tab to adjust your active window, how often Raffi nudges, and which days. Raffi pauses overnight automatically.
