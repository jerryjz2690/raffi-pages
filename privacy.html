Raffi — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Raffi is a hydration reminder app. It is built to keep your information on your device, and this policy explains exactly what that means.

What we collect

Nothing. Raffi does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. It has no user accounts, no servers, no analytics, and makes no network connections.

What stays on your device

Your reminder schedule and your record of acknowledged sips are stored only on your iPhone, using Apple's on-device storage. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or to anyone else.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, Raffi's reminders are scheduled and delivered locally by iOS on your device. We do not send push notifications from a server, because there is no server.

Deleting your data

Because everything lives on your device, deleting the app removes all of its data with it. There is nothing stored elsewhere to request or erase.

Children

Raffi does not collect information from anyone, including children.
